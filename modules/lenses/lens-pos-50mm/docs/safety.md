# Safety — Lens (Positive) 1×1

## Warnings

- **Laser safety:** When used with laser sources, this lens focuses the beam to a small spot. The focused beam can cause eye damage or skin burns. Always follow laser safety protocols (IEC 60825) and use appropriate protective eyewear.
- **Breakage hazard:** N-BK7 glass is brittle. Dropping the module can crack the lens, creating sharp edges. Handle with care and inspect before use.

## Precautions

- Handle the lens with gloves or lens tissue to avoid oil contamination on coated surfaces
- Do not expose the AR coating to abrasive cleaning materials
- Store in a clean, dry environment when not in use
- Avoid rapid temperature changes (thermal shock can damage the coating)

## Disposal

- **Glass lens:** Dispose as glass waste. The AR coating contains no hazardous materials
- **3D-printed insert:** Dispose as plastic waste (PLA is biodegradable; PETG is recyclable)
- **UC2 cube:** Reusable — do not dispose

## Maintenance

| Interval | Action |
|----------|--------|
| Before each use | Visual inspection for dust, fingerprints, cracks |
| Monthly (if used regularly) | Clean with lens tissue and optical cleaning solution |
| Yearly | Check coating condition, replace if degraded |
