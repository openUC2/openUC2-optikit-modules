# User Manual — Lens (Positive) 1×1

## Before You Start

Make sure you have:
- One UC2 cube (v4 or later)
- One lens insert (3D-printed or injection-molded)
- One plano-convex lens (Thorlabs LA1131-A or compatible Ø25.4 mm lens)
- Lens tissue or microfiber cloth

## Installation

1. **Insert the lens into the holder.** Place the lens into the 3D-printed insert with the curved (convex) side facing the direction light will enter. The flat (plano) side faces the exit direction.

2. **Slide the insert into the cube.** The insert slots into the cube along the optical axis. Push gently until it seats against the internal stops.

3. **Verify orientation.** Looking through the +X face of the cube, you should see the curved surface of the lens. The optical axis should pass through the center of both the +X and −X faces.

4. **Connect to your setup.** Place the cube on the UC2 baseplate grid. Connect to adjacent cubes using puzzle pieces or baseplate slots.

## Adjusting Focus

The lens insert allows ±2 mm of axial travel within the cube. To adjust:
- Gently push or pull the insert along the optical axis
- Use a thin tool (e.g., hex key) through the side openings to nudge the insert position
- For precise positioning, use the motorized Z-stage module instead

## Cleaning

- Use only lens tissue or clean microfiber cloth
- Apply a small amount of lens cleaning solution if needed
- Wipe in a single direction — do not rub in circles
- Never touch the coated surfaces with bare fingers

## Swapping Lenses

To change the focal length:
1. Gently push the lens out of the insert from the back
2. Insert the replacement lens (same diameter: Ø25.4 mm)
3. Update the `module.yaml` parameters if tracking digitally
