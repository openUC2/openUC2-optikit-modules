# Safety — Laser 635 nm (CPS635R) 1x1

## Warnings

- **Class 3R laser hazard:** Direct intrabeam viewing causes permanent eye damage. ALWAYS wear OD 4+ safety glasses rated for 635 nm.
- **Specular reflection hazard:** Even diffuse reflections off mirrors can be hazardous at this wavelength. Cover all polished surfaces in the beam path when aligning.
- **Skin exposure:** Continuous-wave 635 nm at 4.5 mW is below the MPE for skin, but momentary exposure should still be avoided.

## Precautions

- Handle the module with care - drops can damage delicate optical surfaces
- Use lens tissue or microfiber cloth for cleaning
- Store in a dust-free environment when not in use
- Always disconnect power before handling internal electronics

## Disposal

- **Glass / optical components:** Glass waste stream
- **3D-printed insert:** Plastic waste (PLA biodegradable; PETG recyclable)
- **UC2 cube:** Reusable - do not dispose
- **Electronics:** WEEE waste stream

## Maintenance

| Interval | Action |
|----------|--------|
| Before each use | Visual inspection for dust, fingerprints, mechanical damage |
| Monthly | Clean optical surface with lens tissue |
| Yearly | Check coating / sensor condition |
