# Safety — Kinematic Mirror (90 deg) 1x1

## Warnings

- **Reflected beam hazard:** A mirror redirects the full incident beam - including high-power lasers. Always be aware of where the reflected beam goes.
- **Glass breakage:** The dielectric mirror is glass-substrate. Dropping the module can crack the mirror, creating sharp edges.

## Precautions

- Handle the module with care - drops can damage delicate optical surfaces
- Use lens tissue or microfiber cloth for cleaning
- Store in a dust-free environment when not in use

## Disposal

- **Glass / optical components:** Glass waste stream
- **3D-printed insert:** Plastic waste (PLA biodegradable; PETG recyclable)
- **UC2 cube:** Reusable - do not dispose

## Maintenance

| Interval | Action |
|----------|--------|
| Before each use | Visual inspection for dust, fingerprints, mechanical damage |
| Monthly | Clean optical surface with lens tissue |
| Yearly | Verify alignment is stable |
